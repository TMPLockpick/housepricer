Features
The AI Advisor is a smart real estate knowledge engine with thousands of expert responses across global markets, investment strategies, mortgages, taxes and more. It uses keyword analysis to match your question to the most relevant answer from our curated property intelligence database.
We currently cover 200+ cities across Africa, Asia, Europe, Americas, Middle East and Oceania. We update and expand the database regularly. If a city you need is missing, let us know in our Discord and we'll add it.
The Address Valuer lets you type in any street address worldwide and get an estimated property value, monthly rent estimate, and a live satellite map of that exact location. It also links you to local real estate agents for that area.
