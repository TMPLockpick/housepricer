# HousePricer 🏠

**HousePricer** is a real-time global property intelligence platform that gives instant access to average house prices across **150+ major cities worldwide**. Designed for investors, researchers, expats, digital nomads, and anyone curious about the global real estate market — HousePricer delivers accurate, up-to-date market estimates all in one place.

## What Makes It Different

Most property tools are locked to one country or one currency. HousePricer breaks that barrier by covering cities across six regions — Africa, North America, South America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia — and displaying every price in its **local currency symbol**, from Nigerian Naira (₦) to Japanese Yen (¥) to Indian Rupee (₹). No confusing conversions. No dollar-only bias. Just real numbers in the money that actually matters in that market.

## Data & Sources

All market estimates are compiled and cross-referenced from the most trusted property intelligence sources in the world including **Numbeo**, **Knight Frank**, **Global Property Guide**, **Statista**, and local MLS data. Every city comes with a full breakdown including average price, price range, price per m², and year-on-year market trend so you always know if a market is heating up or cooling down.
