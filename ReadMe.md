# HousePricer 🏠

**HousePricer** is a premium global property intelligence platform providing instant access to average house prices across **200+ major cities worldwide**. Designed for serious investors, expats, and researchers, HousePricer delivers accurate market estimates, 5-year trend analysis, and a suite of advanced financial tools in one sleek, dark-mode dashboard.

## What Makes It Different

Most property tools are limited by borders or single-currency views. HousePricer breaks these barriers by covering over 200 cities across Africa, the Americas, Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and Oceania. Every price is displayed with its **correct local currency symbol**—from Nigerian Naira (₦) to Japanese Yen (¥)—eliminating dollar-only bias and providing real numbers that matter in local markets.

## The Ultimate Tool Suite

HousePricer has evolved beyond simple search. Your dashboard now includes a comprehensive suite of real estate calculators:
- **ROI Calculator:** Instantly calculate Annual Gross Yield and Net Cash Flow for potential rentals.
- **Rent vs. Buy Analyzer:** Compare the long-term monthly financial impact of renting versus home ownership.
- **Advanced Mortgage Estimator:** Project monthly payments based on home price, down payment, and interest rates.
- **Tax Estimator:** Quickly estimate annual property taxes based on local rates.
- **Affordability Calculator:** Determine your maximum safe housing budget based on gross income and debt-to-income (DTI) ratios.
- **Down Payment Goal Tracker:** Calculate exactly how much you need to save and how long it will take to hit your goal.

## Data & Sources

Market estimates are cross-referenced from trusted global property intelligence sources, including **Numbeo**, **Knight Frank**, **Global Property Guide**, and **Statista**. Each city profile includes:
- **Average Market Price**
- **Estimated Price Range**
- **Price per m²**
- **1-Year Trend Indicators**
- **5-Year Visual Price History Charts**
